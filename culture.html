AI x Culture

How artificial intelligence is rewriting our stories, sounds, and screens.

Movies: The Creator (2023)

A rare film that portrays AI not as a monolithic evil, but as a diverse, sentient community fighting for existence. It visually and thematically explores a future of human-AI integration and conflict, forcing us to ask where humanity ends and artificiality begins.

Books: Klara and the Sun

Kazuo Ishiguro's novel, told from the perspective of an 'Artificial Friend,' is a masterclass in exploring love, consciousness, and obsolescence through an AI's eyes. It's a poignant look at what we demand from technology emotionally.

Music: AI-Generated Vocals

The viral "Fake Drake" tracks and Grimes's Elf.Tech platform have thrust AI's role in music into the spotlight. This isn't just about novelty; it's a fundamental challenge to authorship, copyright, and the very definition of a musical artist.
